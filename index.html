Unique Word Occurences on Breitbart.com's Front Page

May 5th 2020 - November 3rd 2020

From May 5th 2020 to November 3rd 2020, a web scraper parsed every article on the front page of Breitbart.com, and tallied each occurance of 10 distinct words to record their usage rate.
